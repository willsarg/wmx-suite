⚡ Time-to-First-Audio (TTFA) Latency

TTFA measures the startup latency: the elapsed time from initiating synthesis until the first chunk of audio is generated and ready to play. On long paragraphs, streaming yields audio in ~1.3 seconds instead of waiting ~9.5 seconds for total non-streaming synthesis—providing a 7x reduction in startup latency.

Streaming Mode: Audio plays incrementally chunk-by-chunk.
Non-Streaming Mode: Audio plays only after the entire text is fully synthesized.
